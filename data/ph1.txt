# Process History Entry: Order Fulfillment Process

## Goal
Fulfill customer orders accurately and efficiently from receipt to delivery.

## Roles
- Customer Service Representative
- Warehouse Clerk
- Shipping Coordinator
- Quality Inspector

## Activities
1. Receive Order - Customer Service Representative receives and logs the order
2. Validate Order - System checks inventory availability and customer data
3. Pick Items - Warehouse Clerk retrieves items from storage locations
4. Inspect Quality - Quality Inspector verifies item condition and correctness
5. Pack Order - Warehouse Clerk packages items for shipment
6. Generate Shipping Label - System creates shipping documentation
7. Hand Off to Carrier - Shipping Coordinator transfers package to logistics provider

## Dependencies
- Validate Order depends on Receive Order
- Pick Items depends on Validate Order (success)
- Inspect Quality depends on Pick Items
- Pack Order depends on Inspect Quality (pass)
- Generate Shipping Label depends on Pack Order
- Hand Off to Carrier depends on Generate Shipping Label
